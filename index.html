TravelClass – the main platform for booking school holidays

Organizing an exciting educational journey for a school class is a time-consuming task. Group trips involve many stages: choosing a tour, preparing documents, booking tickets, excursions, insurance. The information must be processed in a timely manner and communicated to all participants in the trip and their parents.

Travel Class is an international platform designed for teachers, students and their parents to simplify the planning and booking of school vacations. We offer the widest range of programs around the world, and we make the booking process as easy, online and informative as possible.
