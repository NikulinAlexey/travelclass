TravelClass – the main platform for booking school holidays

Organizing an exciting educational journey for a school class is a time-consuming task. Group trips involve many stages: choosing a tour, preparing documents, booking tickets, excursions, insurance. The information must be processed in a timely manner and communicated to all participants in the trip and their parents.

Travel Class is an international platform designed for teachers, students and their parents to simplify the planning and booking of school vacations. We offer the widest range of programs around the world, and we make the booking process as easy, online and informative as possible.

Four reasons to learn a language in another country

Authentic language experiences

Learning a language in a native country also allows you to have authentic language experiences, such as participating in local activities and interacting with native speakers in real-life situations.

Link to tour

Improved pronunciation

Learning a language in a native country can also help improve your pronunciation, as you have the opportunity to hear the language spoken by native speakers

Link to tour

Immersion in the language

Being surrounded by native speakers in a native country allows you to immerse yourself in the language, which can facilitate learning and improve your skills.

Link to tour

Cultural context

Learning a language in your home country can provide you with an opportunity to learn about the culture and customs of that country, which will help you understand its use in a broader context.
